What is UX?

user experience

noun [u]

- the overall experience someone has when using a product or service.

Contextual Solutions

If your users never use your app on their phones, then there's no need to spend countless hours and dollars on creating a mobile exerpeince. UX focuses on contextual solutions , which makes sure that applications and experiences are developed and designed around how users are using them.

User-Driven Apps

User Experience means User-First. Developers and designers who do UX spend a lot of time focusing on the users who will be interacting with their apps.

Meaningful Experiences

The goal of UX is to create meaningful and relevant experiences. A good UX should be:

  • Usable
  • Useful
  • Accessible
  • Valuable
