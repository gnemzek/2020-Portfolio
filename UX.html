What is UX?

user experience

noun [u] - the overall experience of using a product or service.

Contextual Solutions

Let’s say you’re unsure of whether you need to overhaul and redesign your mobile experience. The answer lies with your users! If analytics show they don’t use your app on their phones, your time and money is better spent elsewhere. Where exactly? Let your users guide that! Contextual solutions ensure that applications and web experiences are designed and developed around how users already use them, ensuring smooth rollouts and increased accessibility.

User-Driven Apps

UX is all about putting the user first, which means taking the time to get to know them. Each site and app has a different user they currently appeal to or are trying to attract, so it’s key for your web team to understand who you’re reaching out to. Once we know your users, and how they presently engage with your website or application, we’re able to let them drive the development to maximize their usage and increase likelihood for retention.

Meaningful Experiences

The goal of UX is to create meaningful and relevant experiences. A good UX is:

  • Usable - easy for the user to understand and navigate
  • Useful - provides an essential function for the user
  • Accessible - relevant for every possible user, no matter what
  • Valuable - succeeds what it sets out to do, providing benefit to the user
